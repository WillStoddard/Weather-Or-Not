Weather-Or-Not!

The Weather-Or-Not program uses a Support Vector Machine to predict "Weather-Or-Not" there will be
a storm in your area. The SVM is trained on historical weather data from your location, then looks
at the recent weather near you, classifying it based on past weather patterns.

If the most recent ten days near you look like the ten days that lead up to a storms in the past,
it's likely that you've got a storm coming!
